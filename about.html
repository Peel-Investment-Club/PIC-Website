about

As students ourselves, we found that the education system lacks in teaching youth fundamental concepts of finance which were paramount to one's future, such as saving for retirement or value investing. As a result, we founded the Peel Investment Club: an organization for students, by students, dedicated to spreading financial literacy among youth. Through our executive team comprised of 15 members attending the top business schools in Canada (e.g Schulich, Ivey, Queens) we prepare and deliver informative lessons to hundreds of students across numerous high-schools in the Peel Region and GTA, along with running competitions and helping finance clubs get off their feet.
