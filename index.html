About Us

The Peel Investment Club, or PIC, aims to spread a passion for business and investing among Peel’s youth by promoting financial literacy and leadership development, primarily through training sessions, engaging competitions and informative talks held by industry leaders and alumni from the top business schools in the nation.

We strive on diversity and inclusiveness, therefore, we welcome all students across the Peel Region to our events, whether you’re an expert investor or are just getting started, we have many resources available to help you in your journey. Furthermore, if you wish to share your passion for investing among your peers we will provide you with the resources and assistance necessary to create an investment club in your school or institution. For more information, look into registering a chapter or view all of the currently registered chapters to find one near you.
