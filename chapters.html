chapters

Chapters of the Peel Investment Club join a network of different finance and investment clubs throughout Peel and the GTA. Receiving access to a plethora of resources such as pre-made lessons and presentations covering essential and interesting topics in finance and investing such as basic accounting, cryptocurrency, real estate investing, business school admissions guides and more. Not to mention, priority registration in competitions and access to an alumni network of students at the top business schools in Canada (e.g Schulich, Ivey, Queens).
